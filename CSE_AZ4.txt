EDU- Admin: 162(162)/2020/2983
Date: 14-09-2020



With a heavy heart, we would like to inform you that, students who did not submit their faculty
evaluation for all their courses and/or didn’t pay their tuition fees, the system will not allow the
publication of their grades.


If your result is withheld, please communicate with us in the following way-

           1.   Students who have not yet completed faculty evaluation, please email us at:
                admissions@eastdelta.edu.bd

           2.   Students are advised to clear their dues before advising of Fall 2020. Students
                who have further queries and requests are requested to email us at:
                advising@eastdelta.edu.bd
Considering the current context, university has considered publishing the results of the students
who have dues below BDT1,000.

Short Forms:

   1. Faculty Evaluation Not Filled: FENF
   2. Payment Due: PD
   3. Faculty Evaluation Not Filled & Payment Due: FENF & PD
